Our Accountability

Service Complaints Against the NFO

The NFO is committed to providing a high standard of service to all parties. If you are unhappy with the service you have received from the NFO — separate from the outcome of your complaint — you have the right to raise a service complaint.

Service complaints may relate to:

  • Delays in acknowledging or processing your complaint
  • Discourteous or unprofessional conduct by NFO staff
  • Failure to follow the NFO's own procedures
  • Inadequate communication or feedback

A service complaint is about how the NFO handled your matter, not about the outcome of a determination. If you wish to challenge a determination, please refer to the options in your determination letter.
