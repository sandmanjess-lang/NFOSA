Consumer Rights

Key rights every financial consumer should know

South African law protects you as a financial services consumer. Here are the most important rights you have.

✓

Right to a written response

Your bank or insurer must respond to your complaint in writing within a reasonable time. Keep copies of all correspondence.

✓

Right to fair treatment

Under the Treating Customers Fairly (TCF) framework, financial providers must treat you fairly at all stages of the product lifecycle.

✓

Right to dispute credit bureau information

Under the National Credit Act, you have the right to dispute inaccurate information held about you by credit bureaus — at no cost.

✓

Right to cancel a debit order

You have the right to instruct your bank to stop any debit order from your account. Unauthorised debit orders can be reversed.

✓

Right to clear product information

Financial providers must give you clear, accurate information about any product before you sign. You have a right to understand what you are buying.

✓

Right to free dispute resolution

If your complaint is not resolved by your provider, you have the right to bring it to the NFO — completely free of charge.
