Track Your Complaint

Enter your case reference number to check the current status of your complaint with the NFO.

Enter your case reference

Your case reference number was provided in the acknowledgement email or letter when you submitted your complaint.

Can't find your reference number?

Check your email inbox for an acknowledgement from the NFO, or contact us with your name, ID number and the date you submitted — we'll help locate your case.

Understanding your status

What each status means

Your complaint will move through the following stages during the resolution process.

1

Received

Your complaint has been received and logged. You will receive an acknowledgement with your case reference number within 5 business days.

2

Under Assessment

We are reviewing your complaint to determine whether it falls within the NFO's jurisdiction and whether all required information has been provided.

3

Under Investigation

Your complaint is being actively investigated. We have requested information from the financial provider and are assessing the merits of both parties' positions.

4

Recommendation Issued

The NFO has issued a recommendation. Both parties have been given the opportunity to respond and indicate whether they accept the recommendation.

5

Determination Issued

A formal determination has been issued. The determination is binding on the participant and they must comply within the specified timeframe.

✓

Resolved / Closed

Your complaint has been resolved and closed. If resolved in your favour, ensure the participant has complied. If you have concerns about compliance, contact us.
