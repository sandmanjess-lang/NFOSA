About the NFO

South Africa's national financial dispute resolver

The NFO was established to give financial services consumers in South Africa access to a free, fair and independent complaints resolution mechanism.

Whether your complaint is about a banking transaction, a credit agreement or an insurance claim, the NFO provides an accessible pathway to resolution — without the cost of legal action.

Our three divisions — Banking & Credit, Non-Life Insurance, and Life Insurance — cover the full spectrum of regulated financial services in South Africa.

The Process

How to resolve your complaint

The NFO process is straightforward, free and designed to be accessible to all consumers.

Full Process Details

  1. Complain to your financial provider first

    Before approaching the NFO, you must first raise your complaint directly with your bank, insurer or credit provider and allow them to respond.

  2. Submit your complaint to the NFO

    If your complaint remains unresolved after contacting the provider, submit your complaint to the NFO — online, by email or by phone. It's free.

  3. We investigate

    The NFO reviews your complaint, gathers relevant information from both parties and conducts a fair, independent assessment.

  4. Receive our determination

    We issue a determination that is binding on the participant. If the determination is in your favour, the participant must comply.
