Complaints Process

Submit a Complaint

Our dispute resolution service is free, independent and available to all South African financial services consumers.

Before you submit to the NFO: You must first raise your complaint directly with your bank, insurer or credit provider. Allow them at least 20 business days to respond before contacting us. If they fail to respond or you are unhappy with their response, the NFO can assist you.

Am I eligible to complain?

✓ Banking & Credit

Complaints about banks, credit providers, financial institutions and credit bureaus registered with the NFO.

✓ Non-Life Insurance

Complaints about short-term insurers including motor, home, commercial and other non-life insurance products.

✓ Life Insurance

Complaints about long-term insurers, life insurance policies, funeral cover and related products.

Not sure if your provider is a participant? View our list of participants or contact us.
