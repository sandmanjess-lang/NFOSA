Real Stories

Case Studies

Real examples — with identifying details removed — of how the NFO has helped South African consumers resolve financial disputes.

Banking

"The bank debited my account for a loan I never applied for"

A consumer discovered monthly deductions from their account for a personal loan they had not taken out. Despite multiple complaints to the bank, the deductions continued for six months before the consumer approached the NFO.

Outcome: The NFO found in the consumer's favour. The bank was ordered to refund all unauthorised deductions plus interest, and to remove the fraudulent loan from the consumer's credit record.

Insurance

"My home insurance claim was rejected after the burst pipe"

A homeowner's claim following burst pipes and water damage was rejected by their insurer, who alleged the damage was due to "gradual deterioration". The consumer disagreed and approached the NFO.

Outcome: After reviewing the evidence, the NFO determined that the damage was sudden and unforeseen. The insurer was required to pay the claim in full — R87,000.

Credit

"I was listed on the credit bureau for a debt I had already paid"

A consumer discovered a negative credit bureau listing for a store account debt they had settled two years earlier. The retailer had failed to update the bureau. The consumer could not get a home loan as a result.

Outcome: The NFO ordered the credit provider to remove the incorrect listing immediately and issue a clearance certificate. The consumer was subsequently approved for their home loan.

Life Insurance

"The insurer refused to pay my late husband's life cover"

A widow was told her husband's life insurance policy would not pay out because he had allegedly failed to disclose a pre-existing medical condition. She approached the NFO to challenge the rejection.

Outcome: The NFO found the insurer had not sufficiently asked about the condition at inception and that the non-disclosure was not material. The insurer was ordered to pay the full sum assured.
