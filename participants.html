For Financial Institutions

Become a Participant

Financial service providers regulated under the Financial Sector Regulation Act may be required or eligible to participate in the NFO scheme. Participation ensures that your customers have access to fair, independent dispute resolution.

Benefits of participation include:

  • Compliance with regulatory requirements
  • Enhanced consumer trust and confidence
  • Access to an independent, cost-effective dispute resolution mechanism
  • Reduction in costly litigation
Enquire About Participation Contact Us

The participation process

  1. 1
    Submit an enquiry
    Contact the NFO to express interest in participation.
  2. 2
    Assessment
    The NFO assesses your eligibility and the applicable division(s).
  3. 3
    Agreement
    Sign the participation agreement and complete onboarding.
  4. ✓
    Active participation
    Your institution is listed as an NFO participant.

Is your provider listed? Submit a complaint.

If your financial services provider is an NFO participant, you can submit a complaint for free.
